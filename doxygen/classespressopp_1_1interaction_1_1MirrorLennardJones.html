Detailed Description

This class provides methods to compute forces and energies of the Mirror Lennard-Jones potential.

where

is the 6-12 purely repulsive Lennard-Jones potential. This potential is introduced in R.L.C. Akkermans, S. Toxvaerd and & W. J. Briels. Molecular dynamics of polymer growth. The Journal of Chemical Physics, 1998, 109, 2929-2940.
