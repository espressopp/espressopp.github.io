GravityTruncated¶

This is an implementation of a truncated (cutoff) Gravity Potential

U = P \cdot \frac{m_1 \cdot m_2}{ \lvert p_1 - p_2\rvert}

where m_i is the mass of the i th particle, p_i its position and P a prefactor.
