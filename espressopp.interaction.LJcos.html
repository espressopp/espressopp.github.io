espressopp.interaction.LJcos¶

if r^2 \leq border_{pot}, then:

U = 4(\frac{1}{r^{12}} - \frac{1}{r^6}) + 1 - \phi

else:

U = \frac{1}{2}\phi (cos(\alpha r^2+\beta) - 1)
